How-to-buy

1) Download TrustWallet & Fund your wallet Simply visit TrustWallet.com & download the application. Purchase BNB or BSC (Binance Smart Chain) to fund your wallet.

2) Enable TrustWallet (iPhone Only) Visit PancakeSwap on your browser. Click connect on the top right corner of the page. Click WalletConnect, and select Trust Wallet. This will open up the app. Confirm on Trust Wallet and head back to your mobile browser.

3) Swap for KMbappe Select exchange from the dropdown menu on the left. Click ’Select a currency’ & enter the contract address. Set your slippage to 20% and click swap!
